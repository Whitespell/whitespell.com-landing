The Team

Meet the team

Pim de Witte

Founder

Pim is the founder and current CEO of Whitespell. Pim founded a gaming company at the age of 14, and left that company in 2015 to start Whitespell after working at Doctors without Borders and Google to fight Ebola. He is most passionate about filling the gap of technology in the humanitarian field.

Koen Vendrik

Co-Founder

Josh Morgan

Co-Founder, CCO

Josh is a Miami native, a true seeker of the heart and inspiring chief commercial officer. He talks to the customers, makes sure they are happy, and builds strategies for products that the tech team builds.

Dave Copps
